Our research work

We develop discrete–continuous optimization models and methods for problems in process systems engineering.

We address process synthesis, planning, and scheduling through novel mathematical programming approaches with discrete and continuous variables (MILP, MINLP, GDP), global optimization, and multi-period models, including uncertainty.

Our work balances theory, computation, and real-world applications.
